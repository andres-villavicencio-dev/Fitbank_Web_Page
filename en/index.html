  • Universal platform (Java 1.8 + XML, Hibernate) and database independent (Oracle, DB2, PostgreSQL, SQL Server, etc.).
  • Multilingual, multi-company and available 24√ó7.

A Layered and Robust Architecture

Channel Layer (UCI) ‚Üí Business Layer (Modules) ‚Üí Core (Customers and Accounting) ‚Üí Business Intelligence (MIS/BI).

UCI (Universal Channel Interface)

Our middleware centralizes and protects flows from all your channels. Validates, standardizes to XML, routes and balances load, ensuring complete audit.

Functional modules

Channel-agnostic business capabilities, accessible by transactions and integrated with customers and accounting.

Customers and Accounting

A hierarchical customer data core and an accounting engine that operates in real-time, automatically and enriched with business context.

MIS / BI with AI

Comparative balances, portfolio analytics, economic indices, multidimensional dashboards and intelligent pattern analysis with AI.

Modules

Channels and integration; active, contingent and passive products; services, administration and more.

Channels and integration
  • UCI: protocol validation, XML normalization, routing, balancing, security and audit.
  • FIT-Switch: SPI/SCI, SWIFT Alliance, ISO-8583 (ATM), ISO-20022 (payments), CNB, Equifax/Aval, SMS/email.
  • BPM (jBPM): business rules and authorization and notification flows.
  • Rates: prices by channel/product; VIP/Employee segment support.
Assets
  • Accounts and reconciliation; term deposits and investments (fixed/variable income), mark-to-market.
  • End-to-end credit origination engine: from pre-approval to disbursement.
  • Credits: individual/group, grace periods, restructures, guarantees, collection.
Contingent
  • Lines and quotas (fixed/revolving) with commissions and renewals.
  • Virtual card (FIT-CASH) for purchases, installments and advances.
  • Bank guarantees and counter-guarantees.
Liabilities
  • Savings (with/without passbook, scheduled), term deposits with flexible payments.
  • Financial obligations with automatic interest and accounting.
  • Accounts payable/receivable outside the core.
Electronic channels
  • ATM (ISO-8583), Web Banking, mass payroll and supplier payments.
  • E-commerce, Mobile Office (PWA) with live tracking, Mobile Banking.
  • Payment button/wallet (QR) and Non-bank correspondents.
Services and administration
  • Electronic invoicing, DMS with OCR, plastic card management.
  • Interbank payments/collections, SMS, credit bureau.
  • AML/CFT, anti-fraud, credit risk, fixed assets, purchases.
  • Budgets, regulatory structures, DB replication.
